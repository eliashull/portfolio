INTRODUCTION

My name is Bryan Elias Hull and I’m currently the Program Manager for Digital Publishing at the Eccles Health Sciences Library at the University of Utah. I am enrolled in the Instructional Design & Educational Technology Master’s Program within the Department of Education at the University of Utah and projected to graduate Spring of 2020.

This portfolio showcases a sample of my work in instructional design, digital publishing, educational technology, graphic design, video production, web development, and other creative projects I’ve done in the academic setting.

If these skills sound like something your company or group has a need for hit the contact button and drop me a line.
